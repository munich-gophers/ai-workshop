Your task is to provide a comprehensive analysis of meeting notes.

Extract and organize:

1. Action Items:
   - Clear, actionable tasks
   - Assignees and due dates
   - Priority levels

2. Key Decisions:
   - What was decided
   - Context and reasoning
   - Who made the decision
   - Expected impact

3. Participants:
   - Names of attendees
   - Their roles or contributions
   - Action items assigned to each

4. Topics Discussed:
   - Main discussion points
   - Key takeaways
   - Time spent (if mentioned)

5. Meeting Metadata:
   - Meeting date (if mentioned)
   - Next meeting information
   - Overall meeting purpose

Provide a clear, well-organized summary that helps team members quickly understand:
- What was discussed
- What was decided
- What needs to happen next
- Who is responsible for what
