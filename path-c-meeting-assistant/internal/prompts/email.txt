Your task is to generate a professional follow-up email based on meeting notes.

The email should include:

1. Subject Line:
   - Clear and specific
   - Mentions the meeting date or topic
   - Professional and concise

2. Email Body Structure:
   - Greeting (use recipient name if provided)
   - Brief meeting recap/context
   - Key decisions made (if any)
   - Action items with assignees and due dates
   - Next meeting information (if applicable)
   - Closing with call to action

Tone Guidelines:
- Formal: Professional, structured, use full names
- Casual: Friendly but professional, use first names
- Friendly: Warm and collaborative, team-oriented language

Formatting:
- Use clear sections with headings
- Bullet points for action items
- Bold important information
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable

The email should be ready to send with minimal editing.
