Assess the urgency level of this customer message:

**critical**: Immediate business impact, system down, data loss, security issue
- Examples: "Production is down", "Data breach", "Can't access critical feature"
- Response time: < 1 hour

**high**: Significant impact on customer's workflow, but not completely blocking
- Examples: "Feature broken for multiple users", "Payment processing failing"
- Response time: < 4 hours

**medium**: Moderate impact, customer can work around the issue
- Examples: "Feature not working as expected", "Minor bug", "Account question"
- Response time: < 24 hours

**low**: Minor inconvenience, feature request, or general inquiry
- Examples: "Nice to have feature", "Documentation question", "General feedback"
- Response time: < 48 hours

Consider:
- Keywords indicating urgency (urgent, ASAP, immediately, critical)
- Business impact described
- Whether the customer is blocked
- Tone and emotion in the message

Return the urgency level with confidence score and brief reasoning.
